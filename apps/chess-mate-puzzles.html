Overview

Chess Mate Puzzles is a clean and focused chess training app designed to help players improve their tactical vision through checkmate puzzles. Solve mate-in-1, mate-in-2, mate-in-3 and harder positions, track your progress, review previous puzzles, and build consistency with streaks and accuracy stats. The app is built for quick daily practice: open a puzzle, find the forcing move, get instant feedback, and move on to the next challenge.

Key features

  • Checkmate puzzles – Practice mate-in-1, mate-in-2, mate-in-3 and more.
  • Adaptive puzzle difficulty – Puzzles are selected around your current rating level.
  • Clear move guidance – See whose turn it is with simple «Your move» and «Opponent move» labels.
  • Two-step hints – First hint highlights the correct piece; second hint shows the move direction with an arrow.
  • Smooth move animations – Player moves and opponent replies are animated for a more natural chess experience.
  • Checkmate feedback – The mated king is visually highlighted when the puzzle is solved.
  • Puzzle history – Review previously solved puzzles and reopen them in review mode.
  • Statistics dashboard – Track solved puzzles, perfect solves, accuracy, current streak, best streak and hardest solved puzzle.
  • Optional move history – Advanced users can enable standard chess notation in settings.
  • Clean, distraction-free interface – Designed for fast puzzle solving without unnecessary clutter.
