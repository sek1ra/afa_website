Gig Driver Tracker

Information We Collect

Gig Driver Tracker may collect or process driving session details, mileage, route history, timestamps, app preferences, and location data when relevant features are enabled.

Location Data

The app may collect location data, if enabled, to track driving sessions, calculate mileage, display routes, and maintain trip history. Location access is tied to tracking features and user permissions.

Background Location

Background location may be used only when route tracking is active or auto-tracking is enabled. This allows an active trip to continue being recorded when the app is not visible on screen.

How We Use Information

Information may be used to calculate mileage, display and review routes, maintain trip history, prepare export-ready records, remember settings, and improve app reliability.

Data Storage

Trip records, route information, preferences, and related data may be stored locally on your device. Storage behavior may depend on the app version and enabled features.

Data Sharing

The app is not designed to sell personal data. Data may be shared only when needed for enabled third-party services, when you choose to export or share records, or when required by law. This policy should be updated if sharing practices change.

User Control and Deletion

You can stop tracking by ending an active driving session or disabling relevant tracking features. If the app supports deletion, you can delete local records using the available app controls. You can also manage location permissions through Android settings.

Third-Party Services

The app may use third-party services for features such as maps, analytics, diagnostics, or other functionality. If such services are enabled, this policy should be updated to identify them and explain their data practices.

Children's Privacy

Gig Driver Tracker is intended for drivers and is not directed to children. We do not knowingly seek personal information from children.

Changes to This Policy

This policy may be updated as the app changes. Updates will be posted on this page with a revised effective date.
