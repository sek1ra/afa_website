Chess Mate Puzzles

This policy explains how Chess Mate Puzzles handles user data.

Effective date: May 23, 2026

App: Chess Mate Puzzles

Developer: Apex Flex Apps

Introduction

Chess Mate Puzzles respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how the Chess Mate Puzzles mobile application handles user data.

1. Data Collection

Chess Mate Puzzles does not collect, store, transmit, sell, or share any personal information.

The app does not require account registration, login, email address, name, phone number, location, contacts, or any other personal information.

2. Offline Use

Chess Mate Puzzles is designed to work offline. The app does not require an internet connection to solve puzzles, use the chessboard, view statistics, or change settings.

An internet connection may only be used by the Google Play Store or the Android system to download, install, or update the app.

3. Local Data Storage

The app may store some data locally on your device, including:

  • Puzzle progress
  • Solved puzzles
  • Streak
  • Accuracy
  • App settings
  • Theme and sound preferences

This data is stored only on your device. It is not transmitted to the developer or to any third party.

4. Internet Transmission

Chess Mate Puzzles does not send your puzzle progress, statistics, settings, or personal data over the internet.

5. Registration and Accounts

The app does not require registration and does not provide user accounts.

6. Advertising and Analytics

The current version of Chess Mate Puzzles does not contain advertising, analytics, tracking tools, or third-party advertising SDKs.

If advertising, analytics, or third-party services are added in a future version, this Privacy Policy will be updated before or at the time those features are released.

7. Children’s Privacy

Chess Mate Puzzles does not knowingly collect personal information from children or any other users.

8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date.

9. Contact

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, you can contact the developer at: apexflexdev@gmail.com.
